About Us

Cleaning services that care

Everclean was born from a simple frustration: booking a cleaning service shouldn't be complicated. We're bringing transparency, technology, and a human touch to home cleaning in the Kitchener-Waterloo-Cambridge region.

Our Story

After spending hours calling cleaning services, waiting for callbacks, and trying to get straightforward pricing, we knew there had to be a better way. Why couldn't booking a cleaner be as easy as ordering food delivery?

That's when Everclean was born. We set out to create a cleaning service that puts customers first: transparent pricing you can see instantly, easy online booking in seconds, and cleaners who genuinely care about your home.

Today, we're proud to serve hundreds of homes across the Kitchener-Waterloo-Cambridge region. But we're just getting started. Our mission is to make professional home cleaning accessible, affordable, and stress-free for everyone.
